- About us -

Located on the bustling Sheikh Zayed Road, Cello Restaurant offers an authentic Middle-Eastern dining experience in the heart of Dubai. For over seven years, we've delighted guests with our exceptional food, live music, and warm hospitality, earning glowing reviews across social media and travel platforms. Each visit to Cello promises a unique experience, with varied seating setups and the soulful melodies of our live bands creating an atmosphere of relaxed joy.

Cello Restaurant

Cello Restaurant is where the middle-eastern heritage of food, fine dining, and hospitality are well presented. Being located in a prominent area, famous for its touristy, and lively surroundings, in the bustling highway of Sheikh Zayed Road, where modern Dubai with its spectacular style, and breathtaking attractions offers great variety, Cello restaurant sites in this cosmopolitan territory, holding an enormous advantage towards the competition. A special place for all food lovers out there. Cello restaurant is truly one of the finest middle eastern restaurant in Dubai.
